The reason I made this is because im tired of having to look at almost every fanfic page to see if a certain esper has been fought or seen. So I made this page so everyone else can know whos been fought just in case they cant make up their mind in thinking which esper they have to fight in the invasion.

I'm not done yet but if you want to help then go ahead...

Relics of the EverlastingEdit

  • Chain Jug (summons Famfrit) (location:Nalbina Fortress)
  • Lightning Horn (summons Ixion) (Location:Seraph has it)
  • Orb of Inferno (Summons Ifrit) (Location:Dante has it embedded onto his sword)
  • Onix of Condemnment (Summons Zeromus) (Location:Rumored to be hidden in some sort of temple or shrine)
  • Dancing Wolf Claw (Summons Fenrir) (Location:AJ Lupine uses it as his main sword)
  • Pyro Scepter (summons Belias) (Location:Tomb of Raithwall)
  • Mirror Ruby (Summons Carbuncle) (Seraph Has It)
  • Ice Queen Diamond (Summons Shiva) (Seraph Has It)
  • Angel Of the Land (Summons Titan) (Location:Seraph will have it)
  • Wings of Besaid (Summons Valefor) (Location:Seraph has it)
  • Blossom Crown (Summons Magus Sisters) (Location:Unknown)
  • Serpentarius (Summon Ultimate Summon: Zodiark) (Location: Unknown)
  • Umbra (summons Anima) (Location: Zane's weapon)
  • The Fang of Cerberus (summons Cerberus) (Location: Zetrra's weapon)
  • Crystal Lotus Flower (summons Yojimbo) (Location: Unknown)
  • Unknown (summons Gemato Soldier) (Location: Unknown)

EspersEdit

Espers are know as the Physical form of a Everlasting. When they die they become a Magicite. When summoner obtain a Magicite they can release the Esper from it Magicite to become a Everlasting in a Relic.

  • Diablos (Relic: Nightmare Blade)(location:With Liz) (Status: Relic)
  • Gilgamesh (Relic: Excalipur) (Location: Unknown) (Status: Alive)
  • Phoenix (Relic: Rebirth Feather) (Location: With Liz) (Status: Relic)
  • Odin (Relic: Gaugnir note: odin's real weapon. Save Zantetsuken) (Location: Unknown) (Status: Alive)
  • Raiden (Relic: Shin-Zantetsuken) (Location: Unknown) (Status: Alive)
  • Brothers (Sacred & Minotaur) (Relic: The Brothers Axe) (Location: Midgar) (Sacred Status: Unknown) (Minotaur Status: Unknown)
  • Valigarmanda (Relic: Tri-Gem) (Location: With Liz) (Status: Relic)
  • Griever (Relic: The Heart of the Lion) (Location: Leading the invasion) (Status: Alive)
  • Bahamut (Relic: Fang of Dragon King) (Location: With Liz) (Status: Relic)
  • Bahamut Fury (Relic: Horn of the Fury Dragon) (Location: With Cloud) (Status: Magicite)
  • Neo Bahamut (Relic: Claw of the Neo Dragon) (Location: With Cloud) (Status: Magicite)
  • Knights of the Round (Relic: Holy Grail) (Location: With Alekssander) (Status: Magicite)

Espers EncounteredEdit

These are the espers that have all appeared in the fanfic

  • Gilgamesh (Status:Alive)
  • Titan (Status:Killed)
  • Knights of the Round (Status:Alive)

Half-EspersEdit

There are some half-Esper in blood. Unlike other Espers, they look human. However they can transform into one, with each a different being such as Bahamut ZERO. Some may even control their bestial powers.

  • Cloud Rain (Inner Esper: Bahamut ZERO)
  • Terra Angelheart(Inner Esper: Unknown)
  • SIN (Inner Esper: Bahamut SIN)
  • Zetrra (Inner Esper: Unknown)
  • Tyranno Pallium (Inner Esper: Tiamat)
  • Despair (Inner Esper: Odin)

Vehicles named after SummonsEdit

For the rest of the Summons that have not appeared in the fanfic make their apperance in forms of vehicles instead.

  • Shinryu (Cloud Rain's motorcycle)
