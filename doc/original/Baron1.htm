"Well, are you ready?," he asked.

"Yes."

"Yes, sir," we both said.

He dismissed us, and we made our way to the castle's bar. The bar held all the weapons, items, and information. There was never a day where new information wasn't tossed around. "Have you heard, Midgar is invading.....Apparently, rebels are attacking some small group from Dalmasca.....Dalmasca is listening to some lady. All I know is that she dresses weird....."

We got our armor on, equipped our lances, and made our way out. "Hold on," I said,"I have to visit somewhere."

I made my way to my house. Ward was making breakfast for his son and nephews. Even at my height, Ward was huge, and used a huge harpoon as his spear. His son took after him, being taller then Cid, Ricard, and Kain's sons, even though he was three years younger."You leaving soon?" he asked.

"Yeah."

"Don't worry," he said, in his always calm demeter,"I'll be just fine." He then looked at the kids, practicing battles."I'm gonna start training them in the way of the Dragoon. I just wish there fathers were alive to do it. It just seems wrong."

"I understand. I'll help you when I'm done, but in the mean time, don't forget: this is what they wanted you to do."I said

He smiled, then went over to train them. I went back to the castle, where Thuree'wun was waiting."Are you ready?" he asked.

"Yea, let's go."

We traveled the whole day, trying to make light conversation. By night fall, we were almost across the border. We decided to set up camp just outside of it.

"So, how'd you become a Dragoon?" I asked, filling in the quietness.

" My dad trained me," he said."He and my mother were the best parents ever. They took care of me, yet didn't baby me, which mademe stronger."

"What happened to them?"

"You know, I have no idea," he admitted."One day, I just got up and left. I trained myself, knowing that the dummies I used didn't fight back. Plus, the Dark Knight center got really mad at me for kicking their asses."

"Nice," I said."My whole family were Dragoons. We fought for Baron bravely."

"Who was your father?" he asked.

"General Alexander."

"What!?" he screamed."So, you are his youngest son? And your brothers and sister?"

"Didn't Avon tell you I was a Highwind?"

"No, just that your name was Xepscern," he said. All of a sudden, a light went off in his brain."So Dalmasca killed most of your family? I'm so sorry."

"Don't be. It wasn't you who killed most of my family, it was Dalmasca."

"This must be pretty deep for you, huh?" he asked.

"Yeah."

All of a sudden, a man walked out. By the look of his armor, he was from Dalmasca.

"Halt," he said. "You two shall come with me."

"YOU!" I screamed."You killed my family, you son of a bitch."

"So you're Xepscern Highwind," he said."Call me Demise. It will be fun killing you."

I screamed angrily, retrieved my lance, and jumped up to attack.
