        Here is the prolouge to my fan fic:

        The world was once a happy place. Peace was throughout the entire world. Combat and Magic were practiced, but it was just for the hell of it. No one ever had a war in their mind.

        Well, almost no one...

        All of a sudden, a war broke out. Queen Branhe, once a peaceful queen, invaded the other three countries from hers, Gaia. At once, a four way war almost destroyed the world. For years and years, battes waged against people for trivial reasons.

        One day, a group of warriors, from diffrent countries, fought together against the true enemy, Choas. Through their leader, Cid Whyte, they learned what really happend. Two weeks before, Branhe went on a good will mission to Dalmasca. On her way back, her ship was attacked by Choas. Everyone wondered how she was the only survivor. Three days after returning, under Choas's spell, she launched war.

        After fighting Branhe, who, even in death, was in a trance, they went after Choas. After ten years of war, Cid launched himself at Choas, and stabbed him, killing Choas instantly. The body decayed in front of the warriors, whose numbers grew, through the adventure, to over fifty people.

        After the fight, with most of the world gathering to hear, Cid made a speech. He proclaimed that the war was over, how Branhe was dead, and that they shall live in peace. He told everyone to live for today, to look froward to the future, but to never forget those who lost their lives in the past.

        In the years past, the three nations of Dalmasca, Midgar, and Baron split Gaia into three equall sections for each.

        Soon, the story turned to legend. The world forgot of Cid and his troupe. For the next years, peace was omnipresent. Again, people praticed combat for the hell of it.

        .|.
