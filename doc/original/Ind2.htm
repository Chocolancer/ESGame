Dante stood up from the cold, wet , ground and looked around and saw no one else but him.

"Damn, im weaponless, armorless, and im lost!" Dante said as he started walking on a path

"At least I still have my magic" he said as he cast Fire on the group of Dire Rats in his way

He kept on walking and killing more dire rats along the way and started getting headaches from being near the bad smells for a long time until he finally saw a door.

"That must be the exit out of this hellhole" Dante exclaimed while running to the door only to find out he only imagined it, So he just kept on walking through the path and walking up and down stairs until he found another door and Dante rushed to it but he stopped abruptly right after a Water Elemental floated up to him and cast Watera on him "So its a fight you want? Then take this!" Dante said as he started to doublecast two firaga spells, but was interupted by another watera spell cast on him, so he quickly cast one firaga spell on him which managed to weaken the Water Elemental but it also made it mad, and it cast Silencega on Dante thus stopping him from being able to cast anymore spells

Dante jumped a step back and used his Wizardly Shout limit break which resulted in two firaja spells cast on the Water Elemental, destroying it

Dante walked up to the door and opened it and ended up in the Lowtown and smiled for not having to stay in the Garamscythe Waterway anymore and left through the second door in Lowtown and walked all the way to the inn and stayed the night there.
