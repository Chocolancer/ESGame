Dissidia Calamitas Infinita Character

Zero, birth-name Cloud, is a character in Dissidia Calamitas Infinita. He fights using two large Zweihander, the first is shaped like an angel wing, while the other is a simpler greatsword.

[edit] Battle

Zero
Dragonic Blackguard – His powerful attacks become even more deadly during Ex Mode.

[edit] Brave Attacks

Name Type Obtained Description
Razor Fang Ground Initial [Close/Mid]Slams both swords against the ground. In Ex Mode it also creates a wave of energy.
Dark Claw Ground Level 5 [Close]Hits the opponent twice with fits. In Ex Mode strikes them with his claws instead.
Additional Effect: Chase
Crushing Lunge Ground Level 22 Quickly stabs with one sword before smashing the opponent downwards with the other.
Additional Effect: Wall Rush
Cutting Edge Aerial Initial [Mid]Throws both swords at the opponent like boomerangs. In Ex Mode they have a much stronger homing effect.
Additional Effect: Chase
Flare Aerial Level 12 [Long]Shoots a homing missile of flame at the opponent. In Ex Mode the projectile breaks guards.
Comet Aerial Level 15 [Long]Three small fireballs appear above the opponent and slowly descend downwards. In Ex Mode the projectile move much faster.
Abyss Spiral Aerial Level 27 [Rise]Spin upwards slightly towards the opponent slashing with both swords. Useful from below. In Ex Mode the blades ignite, dealing more damage.
Additional Effect: Magic Block

[edit] HP Attacks

Name Type Obtained Description
Umbra Blade Ground Initial [Mid/Long]Slash opponent with both blades twice trailing behind dark energy before knocking them away with a final attack. In Ex Mode the attack range is increased by beams of darkness.
Additional Effect: Wall Rush
Gravija Ground Level 30 Powers up weapon with gravity magic before creating a stream of darkness from the ground. In Ex Mode the attack will appear out of the air if cast over a ledge, instead of appearing at next lower platform, and more than one stream will appear.
Additional Effect: Absorb
Inferno Windstorm Aerial Initial Creates a fire tornado next to Zero that slowly homes in on the opponent. In Ex Mode it doesn't last as long but moves faster.
Additional Effect: Absorb
Black Nexus Aerial Level 19 [Dive]Grabs angel-katana and slashes downwards with it covered in darkness, if it connects then slashes more times before knocking the opponent away. Length of combo increases in Ex Mode.
Additional Effect: Wall Rush
Flare Fang Aerial Level 25 [Mid]Shoots several red laserbeams out of sword which home slightly.

[edit] EX Mode

Zero's Ex Mode is Esper Mode. The "Esperkin" ability upgrades all of Zero's attacks.
